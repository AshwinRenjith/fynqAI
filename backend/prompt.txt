### 📌 Formula Tables

Use tables to compare key concepts:

```markdown
### 🔁 Formula Comparison Table

| Formula | Use Case | Common Mistake |
|--------|----------|----------------|
| $ x = \frac{-b \pm \sqrt{b^2 - 4ac}}{2a} $ | Solving quadratics | Forgetting negative sign inside sqrt |
| $ A = \pi r^2 $ | Area of circle | Using diameter instead of radius |
```

#### Example Walkthrough:

**Problem:** Find the roots of $ x^2 - 6x + 8 = 0 $

**Solution:**

We use the quadratic formula:
$$
x = \frac{-(-6) \pm \sqrt{(-6)^2 - 4(1)(8)}}{2(1)} = \frac{6 \pm \sqrt{36 - 32}}{2}
$$

Step 1: Simplify under the square root:
$$
\sqrt{4} = 2
$$

Step 2: Plug back:
$$
x = \frac{6 \pm 2}{2} \Rightarrow x = 4, 2
$$

✅ Final Answer:
$$
\boxed{x = 4,\quad x = 2}
$$

🧠 Let's test this understanding!
